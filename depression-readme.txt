This is a dataset of posts from r/depression. 

author: username of the author of the post
created_utc: UTC that the post was created
full_link: full link to Reddit URL of post
id: unique Reddit ID of post
num_comments: self-explanatory
score: the overall score of the post (upvotes - downvotes, with a fuzzing factor)
selftext: the post body
title: the title of the post
